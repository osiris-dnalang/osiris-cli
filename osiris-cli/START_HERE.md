## Why This Matters

**Typical quantum research**:
- Hope results work
- P-hack until significant
- Cherry-pick favorable data
- Oversell conclusions
- → Non-reproducible, credibility damaged

**Our approach**:
- Lock hypothesis before experiments
- Pre-register statistical tests  
- Design falsification experiments
- Be rigorous about limitations
- Publish negative results too
- → Defensible, reproducible, irreproachable

**Result**: If this works, it STAYS published. Can't be torn apart years later.
